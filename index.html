April 2026 Scrum Summary
📅 April 2026 · Monthly Scrum Summary
ANONotsav 2026 — A Month of Impact & Expression
April was defined by ANONotsav 2026, ANON's flagship event that brought interns, educators, and community members together. Interns across all three teams demonstrated exceptional ownership — from hosting and moderation to video editing, registration desk, fellowship moderation, and on-ground coordination.

Team Connect delivered content, graphics, reels, and supported social media during ANONotsav. The monthly news bulletin was coordinated by interns, marking a first for the batch.

Team Impact powered ANONotsav's registration desk, calling & invites, and also conducted school workshops and attended Anonathan.

Team Expression led ANONTalks sessions (6 talks hosted by Ridhima Singh alone) and Ridhima also served as the main ANONotsav host — an outstanding performance.
Achievements & Highlights
  • ANONotsav 2026 successfully executed — 25th April
  • Ridhima Singh: Main Host of ANONotsav + 6 ANONTalks
  • Ridhima Patawat: 5 reels + fellowship moderation + Anonotsav video
  • Saanvi Prasad: 7 fellowship videos + Anonotsav video support
  • Ekta Ranjan & Aparna Sharma: School workshops attended
  • Monthly News Bulletin coordination kicked off
  • VOM April: 7 interns recognized — Ridhima Singh, Ekta Ranjan, Saanvi Prasad, Devya, Kavish, Angel & Ridhima Patawat
